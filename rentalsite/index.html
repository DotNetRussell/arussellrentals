About

Hello, my name is Anthony Russell and I'm the owner/operator of A. Russell Rentals. It has always been my passion to care for others. After high school I joined the Army and served honorably as a Military Police Officer for 5 years on active duty. This included a 15 month tour in Baquba, Iraq. When I returned back to Ohio I spent some time in the Ohio National Guard before ending my contract entirely.

Since separating from the military I attended Cleveland State University for Computer Science and started a successful career as a Security Software Engineer.

During my time in school I lived in a very poorly maintained apartment on the eastern end of Lakewood. There was one point where I had to withhold rent from the management company so they would fix the water pouring in from my ceiling. Water that had filtered through chemicals and contaminated the only room my son and I had to sleep in. I swore that once I was capable I would move out of there and start my own rental business to compete with this company. I promised that I would never offer appalling conditions like I had experienced. That's how A. Russell Rentals was born and I stand by these ethics to this day.
